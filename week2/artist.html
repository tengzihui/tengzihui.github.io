Jackson Wang is a Hong Kong singer, rapper, and songwriter born on March 28, 1994. He first gained fame as a member of the K-pop group GOT7. Before entering the entertainment industry, he was a competitive fencer representing Hong Kong. Jackson is also known for his successful solo music career and his fashion brand, Team Wang. He is popular worldwide for his talent, charisma, and influence in music and fashion.
